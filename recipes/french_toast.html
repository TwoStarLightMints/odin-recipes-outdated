French Toast

Recipe Credits: alaskan_blue - allrecipes.com

Picture Credits: Ralph Daily - wikimedia.org

Description

A must for those who like their French toast sweet and super flavorful!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon double-strength imitation vailla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground allspice
  • 8 thick slices slightly stale Texas toast

Instructions

  1. Mix whole milk and brown sugar in a large bowl.
