Waffels

Recipe Credits: Megan - allrecipes.com

Picture Credits: Parkerman & Christie - wikimedia.org

Description

A lovely, crispy waffle perfect for the morning.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.

My Own Two Cents

Make sure not to saturate your waffles with too much syrup, because that takes away the nice crispiness of them.
